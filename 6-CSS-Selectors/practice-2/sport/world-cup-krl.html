World Cup of Curling 2018/2019 ( Eng. 2018/2019 Curling World Cup ) - an international tournament men's teams, women's teams and mixed pairs (consisting of one male and one female), conducted in several stages (3 stages and finals) for Season 2018/2019 under the auspices of the World Curling Federation (WCF).
